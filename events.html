How to Find Us

We post all our adoption event locations and details on Facebook, so be sure to look for our events there. When we have a large number of adoptables, we typically show on afternoon weekends on Manhattan's UWS and UES inside or in front of a socially responsible pet store that doesn't sell dogs, cats or bunnies - such as at our friends at Unleashed by Petco.

Our group tends to operate like a roller coaster where our selection varies significantly from one week to the next, especially if we get a "batch" in from out of state shelters and mills. If we have a group of dogs (note that all of our cats are real New Yorkers since there are already so many here in need of homes) coming from out of state we start posting pictures and updates as we prepare them for adoption (spay/neuter, vacinations, microchips and other necessary medical procedures). These posts often first appear on Facebook a few weeks before we have event dates planned. And often we post an event day-of on Facebook! @evessanctuary
