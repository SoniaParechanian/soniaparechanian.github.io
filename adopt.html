Our process

To ensure that our animals are well-placed and don't end up back in the system we follow a process that includes: 1. Application review, 2. Interview/chat with a volunteer, 3. Home visit, 4. Arange for the adopter to meet the animal (if meeting did not already take place at an adoption event), 4. Reference checks and 5. Board review. Once a match is made and an adoption is approved by our board we sign a contract with the adopter, the adopter makes an adoption donation (amount varies by animal) and then we provide medical records.

Post adoption, it is then the responsibility of the adopter to license the animal with the city, register the microchip and establish a relationship with their personal vet. We then look forward to receiving "Happy Tails" pictures!
