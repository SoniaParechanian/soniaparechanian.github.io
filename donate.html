Eve's Sanctuary Animal Rescue

Please Donate

Eve's Sanctuary Inc.is a 501c3 non-profit organization. While we are always seeking forever adoptive homes for animals in need, we are also in need of many other things and there are numerous ways you can help ESAR save lives.

1. PayPal Us! ALWAYS MOST APPRECIATED: You can donate simple monetary donations via PayPal as our biggest expense is veternary bills as we patch up and rehab animals who's medical care has been negleccted. Unlike larger groups, every single penny goes toward animal care and saving lives, as the staff is all volunteer. Just click below! And if you work for a large company, don't foreget to ask if the company matches employee charitable donations as many companies set aside a budget for this (ask early in the year, don't wait until December).

2. Send supplies via iGive! Shop for Eve's Sanctuary Animal Rescue Helped at iGive.com

3. Send supplies or shop on Amazon Smile! You can donate basic supplies (new or gently used) like the following items. These items can be dropped off at any of our adoption events. However, if you are running short on time, you can try our Amazon Smile wish list to streamline the process and have what we most need now sent right to us where we need it!). We update our Amazon wissh list all the time, so check back often to see what we need. If you would like to donate any items directly to the shelter, please contact us.

    Supplies Always Needed

  • Crates and Pens
  • Crate liners/wee wee pads
  • Wet and dry dog food and cat food
  • Natures Miracle enzymatic spray
  • Bleach Spray and Wipes
  • Paper Towels
  • Travel bags and Carriers
  • Pet beds
  • Dog coats
  • Cat litter and litter boxes
  • Non-rawhide dog chews/treats
  • Safe dog and cat toys
  • Disposable cardboard scratchers
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Dawn dish soap
  • Items for charitable auctions

4. Donate a Kuranda Pet Bed directly to the shelter. These are great for the dogs comfort while still in containment during medical treatments or post surgery: Donate to Eve's Sanctuary Animal Rescue in New York, New York | Kuranda Dog Beds. Upon checkout, you may choose to make this donation on behalf of someone else. Simply enter their name and address on the shipping page during checkout and we will mail them a Gift Acknowledgement Card on your behalf, free of charge.

5. Shop our ESAR boutique of carefully curated accessories for your precious pup!
