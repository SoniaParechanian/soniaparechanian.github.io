Contact Us After Reading This...

Email us at to contact a volunteer for questions on how to adopt, volunteer or contribute. Please be compassionate to us we are an all volunteer rescue group and we have full time jobs of our own; someone will get back to you as soon as possible! We do this out of the goodness of our hearts, so be patient.

If you are writing to inquire about adoption and live in NYC please fill out our application in advance and email it to us with your questions.

We operate primarily out of upper Manhattan, and do not have a public shelter that you can visit, therefore, our rescues are placed in foster care as quickly as possible. Our application must be complete prior to submitting further requests for information or meet and greets with specific animals.

The bulk of our adoptions are done in NYC due to logistics. We occasionally adopt outside NYC, but since we require home visits to educate and enhance safety, most of our placements are in, or close to the city, or wherever we have a trained educator/volunteer!
